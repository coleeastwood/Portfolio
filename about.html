WORK ABOUT RESUME GONDOLA

HI, I'M COLE EASTWOOD!

I am a senior at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln, majoring in advertising and public relations, broadcasting, and sports media and communication. I have a passion for photography, graphic design and social media. I have had a wide range of opportunities in the sports industry, serving as a digital media manager for the Hastings Sodbusters, a creative services intern for the Madison Mallards and Madison Night Mares, and a creative media intern for the Creative and Emerging Media department at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln. These experiences have helped me become versatile in graphic design, photography and videography, using engaging storytelling to promote brands online.
