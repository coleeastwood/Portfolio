WORK ABOUT RESUME GONDOLA

I'M COLE EASTWOOD, A CREATIVE WORKING IN DESIGN, PHOTOGRAPHY AND SPORTS MEDIA.

I am a senior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, majoring in Advertising and Public Relations, Broadcasting, Sports Media and Communication. I have a passion for photography, graphic design and social media, and am currently located in Lincoln, Nebraska.

My work is centered around sports and building visuals that feel clean, bold and engaging. Whether I am creating graphics, shooting photos, or helping manage social content, I always want the final product to connect with the athletes and brand to bolster their online presence.
